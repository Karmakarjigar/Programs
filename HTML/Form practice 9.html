Williams College is a prestigious private liberal arts college located in Williamstown, Massachusetts, USA. Founded in 1793, it is renowned for its rigorous academic programs, vibrant campus life, and beautiful setting in the Berkshire Mountains. Williams offers a wide range of undergraduate majors and encourages close student-faculty interaction through small class sizes and a strong tutorial system. The college is consistently ranked among the top liberal arts colleges in the United States and is known for its commitment to academic excellence, diversity, and community engagement.
